This paper extends their previous work \cite{74/dave2018combined} to recommend jobs and job seekers. More specifically, they map job title and skills in the same latent space and then for a job posting/resume, they combine the job title (extracted from work experience), skills and location representations to get a single representation and utilize FAISS [1] for the recommendation.


References:

[1]: Johnson, Jeff, Matthijs Douze, and Hervé Jégou. "Billion-scale similarity search with gpus." IEEE Transactions on Big Data 7.3 (2019): 535-547.
